Gabagool Recipe

Home

Description

Capicola, also known by the slang term 'gabagool' is a type of Italian cured pork, often associated with the TV show 'The Sopranos'

Ingredients

  • Boneless pork neck (~5 lbs)
  • 1/4 cup of salt, 1tsp curing salt
  • 2 tbsp sugar
  • 1 tbsp black pepper
    • 1 tbsp smoked paprika
  • 1 tbsp garlic powder

Steps

  1. Trim pork and remove any silver skin
  2. Mix all spices and rub them thoroughly over the meat.
  3. Place meat in a vacuum-sealed bag or ziplock and refrigerate for 7–10 days, flipping daily.
  4. Rinse off cure, pat dry
  5. Wrap tightly in butcher's netting or cheesecloth
  6. Hang in a cool, humid place (50–60°F / 75–80% humidity) for 6+ weeks, until it loses ~30% of its weight.
  7. Slice thin and enjoy when fully cured
